About KEAT

Keres Engineering and Appliance Technology (KEAT) is a Zambian registered engineering company, established under Act No. 16 of 2011 of the Laws of Zambia. KEAT is dedicated to providing specialized, professional, and high-quality electrical engineering and appliance repair services to its valued customers, both locally and internationally.
